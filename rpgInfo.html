About RetroPi Gaming

During winter break of 2018, I thought of a project to combine classic video games from different consoles, into one mobile gaming console. I began to research what I needed to make the idea possible, and I found a great OpenSource Project called RetroPie which is a linux based operating system. I then decided to choose a Rassbery Pi 3b+ to be the hardware for the console because of it' cost and easy to program. I then began adding my favorite classic games to the console and even added the ability to stream movies and live TV. After winter break ended I decided to use my console for my engineering writing class were I created a Technical Specification Document for R.P.G, as well as a Concept Pitch with two group members. If you want to view those documents press the buttons below. Overall the project was a lot of fun and I learned a lot about creating a Tech Start Up! During the summer I want to begin version 2.0 which is a stand up arcade console.
